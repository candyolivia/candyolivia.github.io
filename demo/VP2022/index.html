Subjective Evaluation

Explanation

  • The purpose of this subjective evaluation is to analyze human perception on number of co-occurrent speakers from a mixture of two voices with same linguistic content.
  • Two voices may originate from two different speakers or from one speaker.
  • Your task it to judge whether each mixture came from the same speaker or not (1 speaker or 2 speakers).
  • We will ask some personal information (name, gender, age, nationality, and your English ability) in this evaluation.
  • We will only use any personally identifiable data to the extent necessary to achieve such research purposes.
  • If you agree to join this survey as a participant, please click the "Agree and Continue" button below.

Examples

  • First, please answer your profile (name, gender, age, nationality, and your English ability).
  • Next, several sound recordings will be provided.
  • You have to judge the number of coocurrent speakers in each sound recording.
  • There are five options for the number of speakers: (1) definitely 1, (2) probably 1, (3) not sure, (4) probably 2, (5) definitely 2
